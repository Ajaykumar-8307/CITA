About The Symposium

CITA 2K26 is the flagship technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science. It serves as a premier platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, innovative ideas, and creative talents.

This year, we dive into the realm of "Future Tech", exploring how AI, Blockchain, and Quantum Computing are reshaping our reality. Join us for a day of intense competition, learning, and discovery.

Technical Events

Debugging

Find the bugs, fix the code.

Rules & Regulations
  • Individual participation only.
  • Time limit: 45 minutes.
  • Languages allowed: C, C++, Java, Python.
  • Use of internet or external drives is strictly prohibited.
  • Judges' decision will be final.
Room: Lab 1

Web Development

Design the future web.

Rules & Regulations
  • Team size: Max 2 members.
  • Theme will be given on the spot.
  • Time limit: 2 hours.
  • Frameworks allowed: React, Vue, Tailwind, Bootstrap.
Room: Lab 3

Paper Presentation

Showcase your research.

Rules & Regulations
  • Team size: Max 2 members.
  • Abstract submission deadline: March 10, 2026.
  • Presentation time: 7 mins + 3 mins Q&A.
  • Topics: AI/ML, Blockchain, IoT, Cyber Security.
Room: Seminar Hall

Puzzle the Process

Logic meets speed.

Rules & Regulations
  • Team size: 2 members.
  • 3 rounds of algorithmic puzzles.
  • First to solve proceeds to next round.
Room: Lab 2
